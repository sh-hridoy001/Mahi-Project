Essential Flower Care

Simple steps to keep your bouquets fresh and your potted plants thriving.

1. Fresh Water, Always

Change the water in your vase every two days. Use lukewarm water and add flower food if you have it. Always ensure the vase is clean to prevent bacteria.

2. The 45-Degree Cut

When you first receive flowers, trim about half an inch off the stems at a 45-degree angle. This increases the surface area for water absorption.

3. Mind the Placement

Keep flowers away from direct sunlight, drafts, and ripe fruit. Fruit releases ethylene gas, which makes flowers wilt faster!
